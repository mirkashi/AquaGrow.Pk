About AquaGrow

AquaGrow is a leading provider of aquaponics systems and solutions. We combine aquaculture (raising aquatic animals) with hydroponics (cultivating plants in water) in a symbiotic environment.

Our mission is to promote sustainable farming practices that conserve water, eliminate the need for chemical fertilizers, and provide fresh, organic produce and fish.
